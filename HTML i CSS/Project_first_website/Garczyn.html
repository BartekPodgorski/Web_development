Garczyn

Garczyn (grosz. Garczińsczé Jezoro) - a gutter lake on the Charzykowska Plain (Kościerzyna poviat, Pomeranian voivodship). The total area of the lake is 113.4 ha, Garczyn is a gutter lake located west of Kościerzyna in the vicinity of the railway line Kościerzyna - Lipusz (station Garczyn) and national road No. 20 from Stargard to Gdynia. The traditions of the camp and watercraft movement on the Garczyn lake date back to the early 1930s. The lake is connected by a narrow isthmus with Lake Wieprznicki. The Graniczna River flows from the lake and is part of the Graniczna-Trzebiocha waterway.
